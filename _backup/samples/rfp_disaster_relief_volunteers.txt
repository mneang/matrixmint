REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Disaster Relief Volunteer Coordination Platform
RFP ID: DRVC-2026-01
Issuing Organization: RapidRelief Network (Nonprofit)
Submission Due Date: (Sample) March 15, 2026

1. Background
RapidRelief Network coordinates volunteers and supplies during local disasters (fires, floods, heat emergencies). We need a platform to coordinate volunteer intake, scheduling, communications, and reporting.

2. Project Goals
- Reduce volunteer onboarding time from days to hours
- Improve attendance reliability and shift coverage
- Provide clear reporting for donors and local partners
- Support bilingual communications (English/Spanish)

3. Scope of Work
The vendor shall provide a web-based platform that supports volunteer registration, shift scheduling, communications, and reporting. The solution shall include administrative tools for coordinators and basic access for volunteers.

4. Functional Requirements (Must/Shall)
FR-01 The system shall support volunteer registration with configurable form fields.
FR-02 The system shall allow coordinators to create events and define shifts with capacity limits.
FR-03 The system shall allow volunteers to sign up for shifts and receive confirmations.
FR-04 The system shall send automated reminders 24 hours and 2 hours before a shift via email.
FR-05 The system shall support optional SMS reminders (if enabled by the organization).
FR-06 The system shall allow coordinators to mark attendance and record no-shows.
FR-07 The system shall maintain a volunteer profile including languages, skills, certifications, and availability.
FR-08 The system shall support role-based access (Admin, Coordinator, Volunteer).
FR-09 The system shall provide a bilingual messaging option (English/Spanish) for templates.
FR-10 The system shall provide a dashboard showing:
      - total registered volunteers
      - upcoming shifts and fill rates
      - attendance rate and no-show rate
      - volunteer hours by event
FR-11 The system shall support CSV export of volunteer rosters, schedules, and attendance logs.
FR-12 The system shall allow coordinators to broadcast announcements to selected volunteer segments (by skill/language/location).
FR-13 The system shall provide a basic audit log of coordinator actions (event created, shift edited, message sent).
FR-14 The system shall support data retention settings (e.g., delete inactive volunteer records after X months).
FR-15 The system shall provide accessibility considerations for web UI (keyboard navigation and readable contrast).

5. Non-Functional Requirements
NFR-01 The platform shall be accessible via modern browsers on desktop and mobile.
NFR-02 The platform shall support at least 2,000 registered volunteers.
NFR-03 The platform shall provide basic error handling and clear user guidance.
NFR-04 The platform shall ensure that exported data can be downloaded without additional tools.

6. Vendor Response Format
Vendors should respond with:
- Summary of solution
- Compliance matrix mapping each requirement (FR-01...FR-15, NFR-01...NFR-04)
- Implementation plan with milestones (30/60/90 days)
- Pricing approach (high-level is acceptable for this sample)
- Risks and mitigations
- Support and training approach

7. Evaluation Criteria (Sample)
- Technical fit and completeness (40%)
- Implementation feasibility and support (20%)
- Data handling and auditability (20%)
- Clarity and quality of response (20%)
